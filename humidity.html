Humidity


The humidity vs latitude comparison shows us that the latitude of each city has no correlation with how humidity the location is. The distribution of the data shows a grouping of the data to be gravitating towards 100% and only a few cities experiencing zero humidity. The important thing to note here is that the distribution of the data here is more distributed across the latitudes since this is one of the axes that we are measuring. In addition, there is no slope to indicate any positive or negative correlation between the different latitudes.
