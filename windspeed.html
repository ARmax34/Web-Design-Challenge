Windspeed


The wind speed vs latitude comparison shows us that the latitude of each city has little to no correlation with how fast the wind is traveling. It should be noted that while the northern hemisphere has more cities experiencing faster wind speeds, they also have plenty of cities experiencing slower wind speed. The northern hemisphere also has more cities since most of the population of the world is in the northern hemisphere. The argument for the lack of correlation between these two variables comes from comparisons of the average wind speed at each latitude. The comparison along that axis shows no to insufficient change to show any trends between these variables.
